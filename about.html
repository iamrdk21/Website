About Us

Phoenix Solar Systems Pvt. Ltd.

Phoenix was incorporated in 1997 & stands tall with the trust and confidence of it's thousands of delighted customers. Within a short span of time the company has established itself as one of the biggest importers of high quality Gas Geysers & Manufacturer of ETC Solar Water Heaters in India. The manufacturing unit located at Satara is equipped with facilities, backed by strong R & D cell and testing labs to produce affordable custom designed Solar Water Heater Systems. The company has all facilities to manufacture and test the products under one roof. Our Management ensures nothing but the best through its state of art infrastructure and professionally qualified Human Resources. The company is now a leading manufacturer of high quality and reliable Solar Water Heaters. It is one of the largest producers of cost-effective Solar Water Heaters of 100 LPD, 150 LPD, 200 LPD, 300 LPD, 500 LPD & higher capacities.
Phoenix Solar is a comprehensive EPC solutions provider for Solar Photovoltaic projects. We deploy world-class technology to design, install and commission solar projects.The highly skilled and dedicated EPC Team at Phoenix Solar ensures the completion of each solar project from concept to commissioning including its operation and maintenance for the entire lifespan of the project.
Our goal is to provide clean, renewable solar energy at affordable pricing and to ensure long time performance by using high quality components.
A team of loyal Distributors and effective supply chain ensures that the end user gets best of the products and services. We at Phoenix Solar Systems Pvt. Ltd. are committed to deliver the best quality products in Water Heating Solutions. It is our mission to invent new affordable techniques to heat water.
